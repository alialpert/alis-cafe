ABOUT ALI'S CAFE

Ali's Cafe was founded in Washington DC by Miss Alexandra (Ali) Alpert. She opened this cafe because she loves coffee, baked goods, and all breakfast foods. She wanted to share this love of hers with the world. In a busy city like DC, Ali believed everyone needed a quiet place to go to, which is exactly what the cafe is.

In addition to our full coffee menu, we serve a selection of fresh breakfast items. We make everything made-to-order, meaning you can customize it however you like.
